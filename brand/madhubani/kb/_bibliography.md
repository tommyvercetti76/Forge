## Citation policy reminder

When a paper is `[paywalled]`, the KB reader should be able to verify the FACT cited via:
- Cross-referencing with an open companion paper, OR
- The book/journal abstract if substantial detail is in the abstract, OR
- The IUCN Red List assessment (always free) for taxonomy + habitat facts

Never cite a paywalled paper as the *only* source for a non-trivial claim. Pair it with an open one.
