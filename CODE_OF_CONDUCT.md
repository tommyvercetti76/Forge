# Code of Conduct

Forge is a small personal project. The rules are short.

## What we expect

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, and any other communication tied to this
  repository. Disagree about code, designs, and decisions — not about
  people.
- Assume good faith. Most misunderstandings are misunderstandings.
- Keep cultural-heritage discussions specifically careful. The Madhubani /
  Mithila tradition is a living folk art practiced by real people; speak
  about it with the care it deserves.

## What is not acceptable

- Harassment, personal attacks, slurs, or sustained disruption of project
  conversation.
- Sexualised language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing other people's private information without explicit
  permission.
- Misrepresenting any output of Forge as authentic Madhubani painting, or
  as the work of any practicing artist.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this policy, email
`rohanramekar17@gmail.com` with `[FORGE CONDUCT]` in the subject. Reports
are read by the maintainer (Rohan Ramekar) and treated as confidential.

The maintainer may remove comments, close issues, block contributors, or
take any other action they consider proportionate to the situation. Repeat
or severe violations result in a permanent ban from project participation.

## Scope

This policy applies to all project spaces — the GitHub repository
(issues, PRs, discussions), any associated chat channels, and any
project-related communication you send while representing the project in
public.
